Fathers & Families

"Fatherhood is not just about being a man; it's about being a family" Fatherhood Program Participant

Being a father is not easy; however, it is the single most important job in the lives of men who have children. The challenges of being a responsible father sometimes seem too difficult and there's no place to turn for help. If you, or someone you know, needs help to better provide for your children and yourself, a fatherhood program can help.

At a local fatherhood program you will find a complete approach to overcoming these very obstacles to become an engaged, responsible father. You will find that the fatherhood program staff understands where you are. At a fatherhood program you will also meet other fathers facing the same challenges and willing to help each other find a better life for themselves and their families.

Parenting

Dads make a difference

Society has slowly acknowledged fathers' unique, vital contributions to child development. An infant only eight weeks old can tell whether a male or female is interacting with them.

This diversity alone gives children a broader, richer experience than children raised by one gender. Generally, mothers and fathers parent differently; so, consider this information as generally accepted by child development experts, realizing exceptions can and do occur.
