About Me

My passion revolves around design and art. I am a designer and a creator with a high proficiency in visual UI design, HTML, CSS3, SASS, social media marketing, English, blogging, & Wordpress.

I have a solid understanding of design principles and typography, and an eye for clean, slick, detailed interfaces.

Education
