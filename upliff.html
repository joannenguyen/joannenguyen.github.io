DESCRIBE THE COMPANY/SERVICE THAT THE COMPANY PROVIDES:

Upliff is a made up app that a team of three others and I created and branded during Startup Weekend in Columbia. It's a good news reading app that allows the user to get caught up on positive news that often gets ignored in lieu of tragic and bad news.

WHAT ARE THE COMPANY’S PRIMARY GOALS/OBJECTIVES FOR THE APP?

To provide a positive social media impact for users and their community. Users should be allowed to create a user account with a minimum of information; probably just a user name, password, and profile picture.

The app should allow participants to:

  • Post their own positive stories
  • Vote up on stories they like
  • Categorize stories based on their content and also if it is news-generated or user-generated
  • Receive notifications from other users

WHO IS THE TARGET AUDIENCE FOR THE SITE? WHAT INFORMATION/CONTENT IS THE AUDIENCE LOOKING FOR?

Any citizens who wish to read daily positive news and who have access to an iPhone.

DESCRIBE THE COMPANY’S BRAND, PERSONALITY, AND CULTURE

Upliff ventures to be what other social media apps are not: positive reading. Compared to Facebook, and Twitter, Upliff would only have positive news and stories to uplift users' experience and days. We wanted an app that users can run away to when bad news flood their other social media newsfeeds. Visually, the brand that we decided on would be peaceful, colorful and engaging. The app should present itself to be a positive experience.
