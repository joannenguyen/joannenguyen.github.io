DESCRIBE THE COMPANY/SERVICE THAT THE COMPANY PROVIDES:

Layr is a applications company focused on accelerating the creation of meaningful 3D printable content. With the Layr 3D Content Creation Platform, Layr is creating a suite of software applications that make any 2D content 3D printable. Since being founded in 2012, Layr's technology has brought meaningful, high-quality 3D printed content to brands and organizations all over the world. Layr is making the world's content 3D printable.

WHAT ARE THE COMPANY’S PRIMARY GOALS/OBJECTIVES FOR THE APP?

As part of their “turnkey” 3d printing service, ZVerse is having to manage a growing 3d artist network. Until now, Layr has managed artist interactions on a case-by-case basis. However, one-on-one management isn’t scalable with new business opportunities. To create a scalable artist network.
